about

Desire Grand Prix (DGP) is a tournament where participants transform into Kamen Riders and compete against each other by defeating the Jyamato and protecting the city. The winner of each game will be granted "the right to bring an ideal world to life" as the prize.

jyamato

The Jyamato are monsters that appear in Desire Grand Prix Game. They are a mysterious and hostile force whose origins and purposes are unknown, but it is said to be a threat to the human world. The Jyamato appear in a space called the Jyamar Area and is said to act according to orders. In order to protect the peace of the city, the Desire Grand Prix being held to target the threat of Jyamato.
